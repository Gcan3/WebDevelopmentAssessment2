ABOUT ME

Who am I?

I also visited other countries such as America and Singapore. I am planning to see other places too like Morocco and Germany.

My name is Gabriel S. Cantiga. I'm mostly referred to as Gab. I was born in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and mostly grew up in the United Arab Emirates. Coincidentally, I was born a day ahead of the UAE's national day. In which case, I could celebrate my birthday during the holidays.

Although I was born and grew up in the Middle East, my nationality is purely Filipino. I can speak up to two languages (english and tagalog) and still getting to know more. I sometimes visit my country on vacations from courseworks. But overall, I have mostly seen and understand the cultures and societies in the Middle East.

My Hobbies

I actually don't do hobbies as often as usual. I mostly commit myself to important courseworks first then I do anything I like to do.
