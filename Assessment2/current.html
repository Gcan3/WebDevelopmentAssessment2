My Current Learnings

Website Development

Developing a website is one of the current subjects I'm studying in the University of BathSpa UAE. Web development is an important aspect when it comes to learning a creative computing skill. Not only does it help me to be creative in developing websites, but it also helps me to fully understand the major concepts of websites.

HTML, short term for hyper text markup language, is one of the main blocks to making a website. While it does not include the styles and designs for its own elements, it acts as the foundation for all the elements used in the website. In fact, without HTML, there is no website.

CSS, cascading style sheet, is another block of code that deals purely with the design and styles of the website. Without it, the website will become crunched together and cannot look as intended as what the coder visualizes. In addition to the stylesheet, animation effects.

Just Like This

Javascript is one of the front-end developments to the website as it gives functioning features that are not present in both HTML and CSS. It deals with a more original and basic coding structure which is also easy to understand. Like the "back to top" button, the javascript allows the website to have more user interaction.

Digital Design

In this subject, I study and learn the concepts of designing. The focus is towards the concept of building a wireframe. Figma is a great tool for making such wireframes.

While the subject mostly lean towards digital designing as per its name, the subject also teaches about the rules and techniques to making a good product design. After all, we cannot afford to make such bad designs.

Click the Figma logo to check one of my draft designs!

Ideation and Creative Problem Solving

This subject is more towards the individual's way of thinking rather than learning the technical skills of computers. It introduces many ways to solve a problem and come up with original ideas. They are called ideation techniques and it helps to become more creative in nature. The activities held in this subject are mostly in groups or duos.

Building on another's work, worst ideas, improvisation, and creative intuition are some of the skills used for creative problem solving.
